Model College Dumka

Model College Dumka is established in the year 2022. Model College Dumka is a constituent unit of Sido Kanhu Murmu University Dumka, Jharkhand. College is situated approximately 9 km away from the SKM University and 4 km away from the Dumka railway station. The college offers education in under-graduation courses in B.A. and B.Sc.

The College offers direct admission to the undergraduate courses as per the directions of the Govt. of Jharkhand and Sido Kanhu Murmu University Dumka.
